# VINSOLAR Calc Pro — Account & Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

1. Send an email to **vinayak.solu@gmail.com** from the Google
   account you used to sign in to the app
2. Use the subject line: "Delete my VINSOLAR account"

**What is deleted:** Your Google account link (email address and
name) stored via Firebase Authentication will be permanently
deleted within 7 days of your request.

**What is kept:** Nothing. The app stores no other user data —
all solar calculations happen locally on your device.
